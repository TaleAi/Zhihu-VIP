## 26.浪漫晚餐：用 have 表达被动很微妙｜讲解
**场景：Joey 看 Rachel 怀孕后很郁闷，就特意充当「男友」带她去吃浪漫晚餐**。


![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-410618b1ab4980c9b90f82faa08b6e04.webp)

**对白文本：**


**Rachel:** Now the filet mignon, what comes with that?


**Waitress:** There』s a side of steamed vegetables.


**Rachel:** Mmm. Now, instead of the vegetables, is there any way that I could substitute the three-pound lobster?


**Joey:** Y』know what? Bring her both, and I』ll have the same.


**Rachel:** Wow! This is shaping up to be a pretty good date—Oh, I almost forgot. I didn』t pay you the rent check.


**Joey:** Oh whoa-whoa-whoa, no roommate stuff. Okay? We』re on a date.


**Rachel:** Okay. Wow! So I get to see what Joey Tribbiani is like on a date. So do you have any moves?


**Joey:** No! No. Umm, just myself and if they don』t like me for—(Laughs.) I』m sorry I couldn』t even get through that.


**Rachel:** I knew it! I knew it. Come on tell me your moves.


**Joey:** Oh alright. Umm, well, okay, I usually start by having a bottle of wine sent to my table from a fan.


**Rachel:** Oh my God. And that works?!


**Joey:** Well it does when you combine it with, "This is so embarrassing, I just want to have a normal life!"


**Rachel:** Oh, you poor little famous man.


**Joey:** Oh okay, how about this one. I was gonna wait until the end of the night to kiss you, but you』re just so beautiful…I don』t think I can.


**Rachel:** Oh my God! Wow! That was fantastic, I almost leaned in. I really almost did!


**词汇短语点睛：**


filet mignon


指的就是「菲列牛排」，来源于法语。filet 是牛身上的「里脊」，mignon 有「小巧玲珑的」的意思（可见这块里脊肉的珍贵程度）。


steamed vegetables


指的是清蒸蔬菜，即蒸煮的蔬菜。


substitute the three-pound lobster


有「代替，替换」的意思，一般的用法是 substitute one thing for another，所以 Rachel 这句后面说全了就是 substitute the three-pound lobster for the vegetables（把蔬菜换成三磅的龙虾）


lobster


lobster 指海里的那种大龙虾，小龙虾的英文是 crayfish（美国英语叫 crawfish）


This is shaping up to be a pretty good date.


shape 有「形状」的意思，shape up 就是「形状逐渐形成」，Rachel 这里的意思就是「今天这个约会看上去将会是个很棒的约会」。  


no roommate stuff


Joey 意思是不谈 roommate（室友）相关的事情，目的是不破坏「约会」氛围。


So do you have any moves?


move 有「动作」的意思，在对话这里相当于中文所说的「（约会）招数；套路」


combine with


意思是「与...结合起来」。


I almost leaned in. I really almost did!


lean in 指的是「靠上去」，Rachel 这句的意思就是「我差点（嘴）就凑上去了。真的！」。


关于 lean in，Facebook 公司的首席运营官（COO）雪莉·桑德伯格(Sheryl Sandberg)写过一本畅销书叫做《Lean In》，中文版叫《向前一步》。


**地道句型活用：**


What comes with that?


come with 有「与……一起供给」的意思，对话中 Rachel 问的是「这道菲列牛排的配菜有什么？」


We』re on a date.


on a date 可以表示「在约会中」这个状态。


I couldn』t even get through that.


get through 指的是「完成；做完」，Joey 这句表达的就是「这种话我实在说不下去/我装不下去了。」


I usually start by having a bottle of wine sent to my table from a fan.


I usually start by doing something 就是「我通常是从做这件事开始的」，Joey 说的就是通常会假装有一个影迷送一瓶红酒过来。


And that works? 


意思是「这样做有用吗？」work 可以表示「起作用」的意思。


**用 have 表达被动很微妙**


本课对话中 Joey 在描述自己约会招数时说 I usually start by having a bottle of wine sent to my table from a fan.这里要特别留意到 have 在这里的用法，句子意思很好理解，就是让一瓶红酒被送到桌上，但往往很难在口语中学会使用 have  something/someone done 这个表达，一是因为中文里不习惯用这样的「被动」表达，二是因为我们没有明白 have 在这里的作用。比如 I'm gonna arrest you. 指的是「我要来逮捕你。」（动作对象是说话者本人），而如果说 I'm gonna have you arrested.指的是「我要（让警察）逮捕你。」（动作对象不是说话者本人），所以当动作对象不确定或不需要特别强调的时候，就可以用到这个表达，比如说「我剪头发了。」，就是 I had my hair cut. （因为一般都不是自己动手剪，而且这样表达好处是你不用去详细说是哪位理发师剪的）。
