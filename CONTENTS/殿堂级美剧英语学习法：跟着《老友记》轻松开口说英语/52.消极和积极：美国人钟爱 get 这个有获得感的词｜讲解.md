## 52.消极和积极：美国人钟爱 get 这个有获得感的词｜讲解
【本课对话取自第八季第 18 集】


**场景：在 Ross 父母的 35 周年结婚纪念日宴会上，Joey 为之前大家嘲讽 Phoebe 男友 Parker 而向她道歉。**


![img](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-e6966cde5e9ee6e455d28cd5d660e522.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Joey:** Hey uh, Phoebe! Look umm, I want to apologize about before, okay? We were being jerks. Parker』s a nice guy and I』d like to get to know him.


**Phoebe:** Then you better do it now.


**Joey:** Why?


**Phoebe:** Because I』m gonna kill him.


**Joey:** What-what?


**Phoebe:** You guys were right. He』s just too excited about…everything. I mean I』m all for living life, but this is the Geller』s 35th anniversary. Okay? Let』s call a spade a spade. This party stinks.


**Joey:** I know. I』m having the worst time. There was a 15-minute line for the buffet, and when I finally got up to the plates, I slipped on a giant booger!


**Phoebe:** Are you sure it wasn』t an oyster?


**Joey:** I guess it could』ve been. I didn』t really look at it. Y』know, I just wiped it on Chandler』s coat and got the hell out of there.


**Phoebe:** I just thought he was such a great guy. I was so excited about him.


**Joey:** Oh hey, you know, you should be excited about him. There』s nothing wrong with him. He』s a good guy.


**Phoebe:** You think?


**Joey:** Yeah. You know what I think? I think we were all just being too negative.


**Phoebe:** You』re right. You』re right, he』s just embracing life. We could all stand to be a little more like Parker. You know what? I am like him! I』m a sunny, positive person.


**Joey:** Actually, you have a little bit of an edge.


**Phoebe:** What』s that now?


**Joey:** Nothing…


**Phoebe:** Oh it』s Parker!


**Parker:** Look! The bunny hop!


**Phoebe:** Oooh I love it!


**Parker:** You do?!


**Phoebe:** Are you kidding? People acting like animals to music. Come on!


**词汇短语点睛：**


We were being jerks. 


jerk 这个词根据不同场合，可理解为「感情用事」、 「笨蛋」、「蠢人」...我们不用严格去记忆中文意思，而是要结合用这个词的那个情景和情绪来记忆，Joey 这里表达的就是「我们当时的所作所为太蠢了，不该那样嘲讽」的情绪。


Then you better do it now.


这里和 Then you had better do it now.意思相同，表示「那你最好现在就做。」，口语中常常会省略 had。


I』m all for living life, but this is the Geller』s 35th anniversary. 


be all for...表示的是「完全赞成；支持」


This party stinks.


stink 字面意思是「有臭味；发臭」，这里引申表达的含义就是这个派对太没劲、太烂了。


I』m having the worst time. 


虽然这里用了 bad 最高级 worst，但实际在口语里只是表达语气的程度，并没有实际比较的用意，就好像老外口语中常说 You are the best.（你是最棒的。）表达的也是赞美和鼓励的语气程度而已。


I just wiped it on Chandler』s coat and got the hell out of there.


wipe 有「擦，抹」的意思，coat 指的是「外套；大衣」。


I was so excited about him.


表示「我之前对他充满期待，非常中意他。」


There』s nothing wrong with him.


他没有什么不对。nothing wrong with 后面也可以跟 something，比如 There is nothing wrong with our products. 表达的意思就是「我们产品本身没有什么（质量）问题。」


We could all stand to be a little more like Parker. 


stand to 可以表达「持有...立场，观点」，这里的意思就是「我们都该像 Parker 一点，向他学习（乐观向上；热爱生活）。」


Actually, you have a little bit of an edge.


edge 本意有「边缘；刀锋」，这里形容人 have a little bit of an edge 就是说这个人的脾气有一点点锋利，可能有时候会发点脾气，伤到别人。


**地道句型活用：**


Let』s call a spade a spade.


英语中的固定表达，意思就是「直话直说」。


You know what I think?  


You know what I think? 这句也是口语中常见的「自问自答」式的表达，看似没有信息量，实际是起到口语中润滑剂的作用，后面就直接抛出他想说的话。


**美国人钟爱 get 这个有获得感**的词


本课对话开头，Joey 说 Parker』s a nice guy and I』d like to get to know him. 美国人非常钟爱使用 get to，Joey 这句话也可以说成 I』d like to know him.，不过和 get to know him 的意思还是有思维差别，用 get to do something 表达的意思是「能够有机会做某事」，这句就是表示「能有机会认识他", 另外 get to do something 还可以表示「越来越；逐渐」，比如 I』m getting to know him.(我越來越了解他了。)
