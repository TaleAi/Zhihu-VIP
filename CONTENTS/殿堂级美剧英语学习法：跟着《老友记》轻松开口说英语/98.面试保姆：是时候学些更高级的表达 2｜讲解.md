## 98.面试保姆：是时候学些更高级的表达 2｜讲解
【本课对话取自第九季第 6 集】


**场景：Rachel 和 Ross 想提前找保姆照顾 Emma，没想到面试中最符合标准的是个男的。**


![img](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-c1b7ba2a51c2121f6f0ac7ff7f1af97b.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Rachel:** So I don't go back to work for another four weeks, but we would like our nanny to start right away, so that Emma could get a chance to know her.


**Nanny Candidate:** I think that's really smart. The easier we can make the transition for her, the better.


**Rachel:** That's great, great. So do you have any questions for us?


**Nanny Candidate:** Not really.


**Rachel:** All right. Well thank you so much for coming...


**Ross:** Thank you.


**Rachel:** Really nice to meet you... and we'll call you.


**Nanny Candidate:** Oh, you know, wait. I do have one question. Do you guys do random drug testing?


**Ross:** Boy, we uhm... hadn't really thought of that.


**Nanny Candidate:** That's cool. But... but if you do, I'm gonna need three days』 notice.


**Rachel:** Okidoki! Wow! We're never gonna find a nanny.


**Ross:** Oh, come on Rach, we will. I promise. We have more interviews. And worse comes to worst, we can always reconsider the uhm... the first one we met with.


**Rachel:** What, the blonde with no bra?


**Ross:** She was blonde? Just a sec.! Okay, okay. This one's name is Sandy. She's got a degree in early childhood education, uhm... she worked for her last family for three years.


**Rachel:** Okay...


**Sandy:** Hi... I'm Sandy.


**Ross:** And she's a little mannish...


 


**词汇短语点睛：**


I don't go back to work for another four weeks.


这句意思是「我四周以后才会回去上班。」相当于 I will go back to work in four weeks.


 


We would like our nanny to start right away, so that Emma could get a chance to know her.


nanny 指的是「保姆」，start right away 就是「立刻开始（工作）」。


 


I do have one question.


do 这里表示强调，表示「我真的是有一个问题。」


 


Do you guys do random drug testing?


random drug testing 指的是「随机的毒品测试」。


 


Boy, we uhm... hadn't really thought of that.


这里 Ross 表示「我们还真没想到过这个。」


 


Okidoki! 


Okidoki 就是 Okey 在口语里面比较俏皮的说法，还有一种拼写是 Okie dokie。


 


Just a sec.


sec 就是 second 的缩写，这句意思就是「稍等一下」。


 


She's got a degree in early childhood education, uhm... she worked for her last family for three years.


early childhood education 有「早期儿童教育」的含义，在美国通常指的是针对 0-10 岁孩子的教育。last family 指的就是「之前服务的那个家庭」。


  



And she's a little mannish...


mannish 通常指的是女子「像男人的，男性化的」。


 


**地道句型活用：**


The easier we can make the transition for her, the better.


前半句如果用大家习惯的句式就是 We can make the transition easier for her，这句中把 easier 放在句首，其实就是为了强调。


 


And worse comes to worst, we can always reconsider the uhm... the first one we met with.


worse comes to worst 可以表示「如果最坏的事情；发生最糟的情况」，这里指的最坏情况就是后面的面试者都不合适。


 


**是时候学些更高级的表达（二）**


本期来聊聊 notice 这个词，本课对话中保姆面试者说到 But... but if you do, I'm gonna need three days' notice. 注意这里 notice 指的是「事先通知」。这里她的意思就是「如果要做毒品测试，你们要提前 3 天通知我。」这句用的是 need three days' notice，对于 Ross 和 Rachel 来说就是相当于 give her three days' notice. 


如果是提前很短时间通知某人要做某事（可能来不及准备），这样的通知就叫做 short notice(注意很晚通知某人不是 late notice)。例如


I can't cancel my plans at such short notice. （我无法在这么短的时间内取消我的安排。）


另外，如果要表达「到了最后一刻，才临时来的通知」可以用 on a moment』s notice，比如


He』s a great doctor. He』s always there on a moment』s notice. （他真是个好医生。他总是随传随到。）
