## 62.备受打击：先否定再肯定，表达有力量｜讲解
【本课对话取自第八季第 21 集】


**场景：Monica 的餐厅在当地报纸上的评价很差，Ross 买下了周围所有报亭的报纸，Monica 看到后备受打击，朋友们纷纷安慰她。**


![img](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-c54d969a09e124be87ab2c103de68fa4.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Ross**: Hey you guys I got some bad news.


**Phoebe**: Well that』s no way to sell newspapers. Why don』t you try, "Extra! Extra! Read all about it!"


**Ross**: No, Monica』s restaurant got a horrible review in the Post. I didn』t want her to see it, so I ran around the neighborhood and bought all the copies I could find.


**Joey**: Man, this is bad! And I』ve had my share of bad reviews. I still remember my first good one though. "Everything else in this production of Our Town was simply terrible. Joey Tribbiani was abysmal."


**Monica**: Hey!


**Chandler**: Hey.


**Monica**: Oh my God! Look at all the newspapers! It must be a good review! Is it great?!


**Ross**: Umm…


**Monica**: Oh dear God!


**Ross**: But the good news is, no one in a two-block radius will ever know.


**Monica**: What about the rest of Manhattan?!


**Ross**: Yeah, they all know.


**Monica**: Oh my God, this is horrible!


**Chandler**: I』m so sorry.


**Monica**: I』m so humiliated!


**Rachel**: Yeah but y』know what they say Mon, "There』s no such thing as bad press."


**Monica**: You don』t think that umm, "The chef』s Mahi Mahi was awful awful," is bad press?


**Rachel**: I didn』t write it.


**Monica**: Is he right? Am I really—Am I awful?


**All**: No!


**Joey**: Hey! Hey Monica! You listen to me, okay? And I』m not just saying this 『cause I』m your friend, I』m sayin』 it 『cause it』s the truth. Your food is abysmal!


**词汇短语点睛：**


Extra! Extra! Read all about it!


对应中文就是「号外！号外！快来看啊！」Extra!表示「号外」，指的就是对发生的重大新闻和特殊事件，为迅速及时地向读者报道而临时编印的报刊，也有「特别报道」、「特别消息」的意思，类似英文里的 breaking news。


Monica』s restaurant got a horrible review in the Post.


review 指的就是「评论」，the Post 指的是「邮报」，例如《华盛顿邮报》（The Washington Post），《纽约邮报》（The New York Post）。


I ran around the neighborhood and bought all the copies I could find.


neighborhood 既可以指居住在家周围区域里的人,即街坊邻居，也可以指家周围的区域,。copy 可以表示「一份」，a copy of... 表示「一份...」，copies 就是「多份」。


I』ve had my share of bad reviews.


had my share of 表达的字面意思就是「我的一份已经经历了。」，这句 Joey 说的就是「我也被批的很厉害。」


I still remember my first good one though. 


注意这里句尾 though 的用法，表示情绪上的转折，Joey 表达的就是虽然一直被批，「但我仍然记得我第一个好的评论。」


Everything else in this production of Our Town was simply terrible. Joey Tribbiani was abysmal.


production 除了表示「生产」，也可以指电影、音乐等作品的「制作；制片」，abysmal 可以表示「一塌糊涂；糟透了的」，这里显然是 Joey 并不知道 abysmal 的意思，他还以为这是个褒义词。


But the good news is, no one in a two-block radius will ever know.


radius 有「半径」的意思，也可以表示「周围；范围」，Ross 这句的意思就是「但好消息是方圆两条街内没有人会知道。」


**地道句型活用：**


I』m so sorry.


sorry 除了表达「抱歉」，也可以表示「遗憾」。这句的含义就是 I'm sorry to hear that. 表示一种安慰，即「很遗憾/很难过听到这些东西。」 


There』s no such thing as bad press.


意思是「没有所谓的负面新闻。」there's no such thing/person as...意思就是「沒有...这样的东西/人。"比如：There』s no such thing as a free lunch.（天下没有免费的午餐。）


**先否定再肯定，表达有力量**


本课对话末尾，Joey 说 And I』m not just saying this 『cause I』m your friend, I』m sayin』 it 『cause it』s the truth. 这里要注意 I』m not just saying this...I』m sayin』 it...这样的表达，这句的意思就是「我这么说不是因为我是你朋友，而是因为这是事实。」 这种先否定再肯定的表达会让语气很有力量。
