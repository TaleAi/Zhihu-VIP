## 58.见怪不怪：make it 用法真是多｜讲解
【本课对话取自第八季第 20 集】


**场景：Monica 和 Phoebe 帮 Rachel 筹备 baby** **shower，结果却忘了通知 Rachel 的妈妈。**


![img](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-9f1ec18d6c41f8122b6f833e7f3f875d.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Rachel:** Hi!


**Monica**: Hey!


**Phoebe**: Hey!


**Rachel:** So what』s the final head count on my baby shower?


**Phoebe:** About twenty, a couple people from work who had something else to do.


**Monica:** Also both of your sisters called and neither can make it.


**Rachel:** What?! You mean they』re not coming to a social event where there』s no men and no booze?! That』s shocking! I don』t care, as long as my mom』s here.


**Monica:** Oh my God, your mother!


**Rachel:** What?! My mom』s not gonna be here?!


**Monica:** Well, given that we forgot to invite her it would be an awfully big coincidence if she was.


**Rachel:** My God!


**Monica:** Well it wasn』t my fault. Phoebe was in charge of the invitations!


**Phoebe:** Well I don』t, I don』t have a mother so often I forget that other people…


**Monica:** Oh give it a rest!


**Rachel:** So my mother is not coming to my baby shower?!


**Phoebe:** No. Neither is mine.


**Monica:** Okay, y』know what? Don』t worry, okay? We』ll take care of it. We』ll call her. You just go home and get ready.


**Rachel:** Please, make sure she comes. That』s really important to me, I mean that』s my mom!


**Phoebe:** I know. I know, what』s her number?


**Rachel:** I don』t know.


**Monica:** Go! I have it in my book. Go! Wait a minute! If you』re in charge of the invitations why am I the one who has to call her?


**词汇短语点睛：**


head count


count 做动词表示「数数；计算总数」，这里是做名词，head count 就是总人数 ，人头数。


baby shower


baby shower 指专门为准妈妈和即将出生的第一个宝宝准备的庆祝 party，shower 这个词在这里所代表的是准妈妈「be showered with」各种各样的小礼物，一般都是一些尿布、奶瓶、小毯子、小衣服等非常实用的宝宝用品。 


booze


booze 是比 alcohol 更加口语化的表达，表示「酒」。


I don』t care, as long as my mom』s here.


as long as 表示「只要...…就行」。


Well, given that we forgot to invite her it would be an awfully big coincidence if she was.


given that...表示「鉴于、考虑到」，这句的意思就是「考虑到我们忘了邀请她，她要是能来那还真是天大的巧合呢。」


I have it in my book.


这里的 book 指的是 address book 即通讯录/电话簿。


If you』re in charge of the invitations why am I the one who has to call her?


意思是「既然你是负责邀请来宾，那为什么是我打电给她？


**地道句型活用：**


Oh give it a rest!


give it a rest 字面意思是「让它休息休息」 ，在口语中的含义是「少来这一套；得了，别再说了！」


We』ll take care of it.


这句意思是「我们会处理好的。」，另一种表达是 It will be taken care of.


**make** **it 用法真是多**


本课对话中，Monica 说到一句 Also both of your sisters called and neither can make it.（你的妹妹们打电话来说她们没空过来。），make it 在口语中经常使用，make 有"做成"的意思，这句中表达的就是「设法做到某事」。make it 的一些常见用法如下：


表示达到目标/做成功：


I believe that you'll make it.


我相信你能做到。 


表示及时赶到


The bus will leave in five minutes, I don't think we can make it.


汽车 5 分钟后就发车了，我觉得我们赶不上了。


表示病情好转


The doctor said he couldn't make it. 


医生说他没什么希望了。 


表示约定时间


Let』s make it at 6:30.


我们约定在 6 点半吧。
