## 96.夫妻吵架：是时候学些更高级的表达 1｜讲解
【本课对话取自第九季第 5 集】


**场景： Monica 和 Chandler 两人计划生小孩，Monica 因为 Chandler 再次抽烟而大发雷霆。**


![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-2c7f6494b717a71df6cb5fcebc242e65.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Monica:** Spend more time with the tie. That'll make a baby.


**Chandler:** Look, I can't do this. I can't make love to you while we're fighting this way.


**Monica:** Oh sure, now you're Mister Sensitivity. But when you wanted to have sex right after my uncle's funeral...


**Chandler:** That was a celebration of life! Alright, look, I'm not gonna do this. Alright, is this really the way you want our baby to be conceived?


**Monica:** No, you're right. We shouldn't do it like this. Huh. For what it's worth, I'm, I'm sorry. I shouldn't have come down on you so hard about the smoking. So you had a few cigarettes, it's not the end of the world.


**Chandler:** Really?


**Monica:** Yeah.


**Chandler:** You are incredible. Listen, I'm not gonna smoke again. And if I do, I promise, I will hide it so much better from you.


**Monica:** Do you want to?


**Chandler:** Yeah, let's celebrate life!


**Monica:** Okay.


 


**词汇短语点睛：**


Spend more time with the tie.


比较常见的用法是 spend time with sb（某人一起度过时光；花时间和某人在一起），这里 Monica 是有点讽刺 Chandler，因为他一直在弄他的领带。


 


I can't make love to you while we're fighting this way.


fight 既可以指口头的「吵架」，也可以是肢体上的「打架」。在男女关系中，通常像这句里的用法来表示「吵架」。


 


Oh sure, now you're Mister Sensitivity.


英文中常见用 Mister（Mr.）...来形容一个人， 这里 Monica 就是讽刺 Chandler 是「敏感先生」。


 


But when you wanted to have sex right after my uncle's funeral...


right after 这里表示「就在…之后」。


 


I shouldn't have come down on you so hard about the smoking.


come down on sb 口语中表示「斥责；训责」的意思。


 


So you had a few cigarettes, it's not the end of the world.


have a cigarette 可以表示「抽烟」，这里 Monica 的意思就是「之前你抽了几次烟，不是什么大不了的事情。」


 


And if I do, I promise, I will hide it so much better from you.


hide sth from sb 意思是「藏起某物不让某人知道」，这里 it 指的是抽烟这件事情。


 


**地道句型活用：**


For what it's worth


字面意思是「不管有用与否，无论价值如何」主要用于口语，表示你不确定你告诉别人的事情是否有用，表达的含义就是「我所说的只是个人意见，不一定对你有帮助，听不听完全由你。」就像铺了个台阶，让双方都能避免尴尬。


 


**是时候学些更高级的表达（一）**


关于怀孕相关的表达，之前的课程中学到过 have a baby 可以用来表示「生小孩」，表达某人已怀有 6 个月身孕，可以说 6 months pregnant。在本课对话中，我们学到了另一个表达，Chandler 说到一句 Alright, is this really the way you want our baby to be conceived?这句中的 conceive 表达的是动作「怀上；受孕」，比如 The baby was conceived in March, so will be born in December.（这孩子是 3 月份怀上的，所以会在 12 月出生。）注意和 pregnant 的区别，pregnant 表达的是「怀孕「的状态。
