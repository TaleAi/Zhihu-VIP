## 42.灵魂伴侣：相似句子对比学习，事半功倍｜讲解
**场景：Phoebe、Rachel 和 Chandler 在 Central Perk 咖啡馆就「灵魂伴侣」的话题聊了起来，Phoebe 说昨晚和她约会的人和 Monica 很配。**


![img](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-2fafb32e494c7ca4c96f7ea6390f7746.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Phoebe:** Oh hey you guys!


**Chandler**: Oh hey!


**Rachel**: Hi!


**Phoebe:** Hey listen let me ask you, do you believe in soul mates?


**Rachel:** Oh yes I do. I do. I believe that there is one perfect person out there for everyone. And you know how you find him? You stop looking for him. That』s why I stopped looking for Russell Crowe. He』ll find me.


**Phoebe:** Uh-huh, (To Chandler) and you?


**Chandler:** No. I mean I believe that uh, certain people are more suited for each other and I believe in falling in love, but soul mates, I don』t think they exist.


**Phoebe:** Okay, good.


**Chandler**: Why?


**Phoebe:** Well last night, I met Monica』s.


**Chandler**: What?


**Phoebe:** Yeah, I had a date with this guy, and I swear to God, he is her other half.


**Chandler:** Come on, don』t be crazy. (To Rachel) You don』t think there』s somebody out there better suited for Monica than me, do ya? (Rachel looks at him.)


**Rachel:** (To Phoebe) Well, what』s he like?!


**Phoebe:** Well he』s tall.


**Rachel:** Uh-huh.


**Phoebe:** He has brown hair.


**Rachel:** Of course, of course.


**Chandler:** A tall guy with hair similar to mine, oh unknowable universe!


**Phoebe:** He works with food!


**Rachel:** Oh sure. Older?


**Phoebe:** Obviously, and he』s British.


**Rachel:** Oh, I was just gonna ask!


**Phoebe:** Yeah? And, he』s-he』s so centered and mature and confident.


**Rachel:** Oh, it』s so sad they never had a chance to meet.


**Chandler:** Luckily, the guy she settled for can』t hear what you』re talking about.


**Phoebe:** Oh, I』m so sorry. Um, and maybe I』m wrong! I but—y』know I』m gonna go out with him again, I』ll find out more.


**Chandler:** You believe that this guy is destined for someone else and you』re still gonna date him?


**Phoebe:** Well, he may not be my soul mate, but a girl』s gotta eat. 


**词汇短语点睛：**


I believe that there is one perfect person out there for everyone.


Rachel 说的是每一个人都会有一个完美的人和其相配，这里要注意 out there 这个表达，老外在说的时候脑海中浮现的是「外面的世界」即咖啡屋以外的大千世界。


You stop looking for him. 


这句话 Rachel 说出了自己认为找到 soul mate 的方法，就是「不去找」。


That』s why I stopped looking for Russell Crowe. 


注意这里 Rachel 说 stop 的时候用了过去式，表示的意思是我当时没有去找 Russell Crowe，Russell Crowe 是曾主演《角斗士》、《美丽心灵》的男演员，显然 Rachel 当时心目中的 soul mate 就是他。


I believe that uh, certain people are more suited for each other.


Chandler 觉得某些男女之间，夫妻之间，相较其他人来说，他们彼此之间更加合适。


I believe in falling in love, but soul mates, I don』t think they exist.


这句 Chandler 补充到「我相信'相爱'这件事儿，至于灵魂伴侣，我不觉得有这样的一回事儿。」


Yeah, I had a date with this guy, and I swear to God, he is her other half.


之前提到了 last night，所以这里 Phoebe 做了省略，说全了是 I had a date with this guy last night. 后面她说 he is her other half 就是说「跟我约会的这个人绝对是 Monica 的另一半」，这里 other half 就是 soul mate 的意思，Phoebe 认为他和 Monica 太配了。


He works with food!


work with 可以表达「和...打交道」的意思，这里说的就是「他是和食物、和吃的东西打交道」，即从事「食物、食品」这个行业的。（Monica 的职业是厨师，所以 Phoebe 就是举例说明这个男的和 Monica 很配）


And, he』s-he』s so centered and mature and confident.


centered 指的是「情感上稳定，有安全感」，可以对应中文里的「稳重」。


Luckily, the guy she settled for can』t hear what you』re talking about.


这句话意思「还好她老公听不到你们之间说什么。」settle 有「安排好；定居」的含义，可以和 down 搭配，表示「安顿下来」，settle for 一般是表示出于无奈或没有更好的选择，最终 settle 下来和某事或和某人一起，句子中指的就是 the guy 即 Chandler。


I』m gonna go out with him again.


go out with...字面意思是「和某人出去」，实际表达的就是男女之间的约会，交往等，在美国文化里，男女之间要多次约会，确定双方是合适的人之后，才会正式确定这段关系，而在这种暧昧期的约会就可以用 go out。


You believe that this guy is destined for someone else and you』re still gonna date him?


Chandler 这里的意思就是「Phoebe 你明明觉得他跟别人（Monica）是天生一对，你还要跟他约会？」be destined for 就是表示「命中注定和某人在一起」， date 作动词可以表示「和...约会」。


Well, he may not be my soul mate, but a girl』s gotta eat. 


字面意思是「虽说他不是我的灵魂伴侣，但我总得吃饭吧。」，言外之意就是「我总也要有钱花，有人养着吧。」


**地道句型活用：**


Do you believe in soul mates?


这句 Phoebe 问的是「你们相信有灵魂伴侣吗？」，believe in 和 believe 相比，「相信」的程度更深，believe in 往往表示「信仰；信任」之意。其后的常用搭配语为：有关宗教、观念、原则、概念及可信任之人等。比如「你相信真的有鬼吗？」英文就是 Do you believe in ghosts?


You don』t think there』s somebody out there better suited for Monica than me, do ya?


注意这里 you don't...do ya?这个句式，要结合 Chandler 说这句话的语境、语气来学习，他想表达的意思就是「你不会觉得会有人比我更适合 Monica 吧？」，better suited 表示「更加合适」，另外这句话中不要忽略 out there 这种小词。


**相似句子对比学习，事半功倍**


本课对话中，Rachel 说到一句 I was just gonna ask!，意思是「我刚正准备问你呢！」，这句话看似非常简单，但要能真正掌握，脱口而出还需要每一次结合说话人的情绪和场景反复练习，而不是简单的重复朗读。最好的方法就是以后你再碰到类似 was gonna...这样的句型的时候，能总结在一起，比如：


**I knew something like this was gonna happen.**


我之前就知道这种事是可能会发生的。


**She didn't say there was gonna be a party.**


她之前没说会有一个派对。


**I was gonna propose tonight.**


我本来打算今晚求婚的。


像这样相互对比练习，体会每个句子说话人的情绪和表达的含义，久而久之你在表达过去那个动作画面的时候，就能正确地触发说出 was gonna...这个句式。
