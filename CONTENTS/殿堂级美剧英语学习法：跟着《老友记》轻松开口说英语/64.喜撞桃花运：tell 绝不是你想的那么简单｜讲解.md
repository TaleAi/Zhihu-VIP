## 64.喜撞桃花运：tell 绝不是你想的那么简单｜讲解
【本课对话取自第八季第 21 集】


**场景：Ross 陪 Rachel 去婴儿用品商店买东西，商店的收银员对 Ross 极有好感并对其献媚。**


![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-eede65a3db105e980e087e268eed64a8.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Cashier:** Do you uh, want these things delivered Mr. and Mrs. Geller?


**Rachel**: Oh.


**Ross**: Oh.


**Rachel:** No-no-no! No, no, no, we』re not married.


**Ross:** We are having a baby together, but we』re not involved. I mean, uh we-we were seeing each other a while ago, but then we were just friends. And then there was one drunken night. Or, yes stranger, we』d like this delivered please.


**Cashier:** Why don』t you fill out this address card.


**Ross:** Oh, okay.


**Cashier:** I noticed you picked out a lot of our dinosaur items.


**Rachel:** Oh yeah! Actually, that』s one of the reasons why we』re not a couple.


**Ross:** I chose those. I』m a paleontologist.


**Cashier:** Really?! That is so cool!


**Rachel:** Oh. Oh yeah, don』t get too worked up over it. I mean it-it sounds like he』s a doctor, but he』s not.


**Cashier:** Oh no-no, I』m fascinated by paleontology. Have you read the new Walter Alvarez book?


**Ross:** Yeah! I-I teach it in my class.


**Rachel:** Oh my God! I』m standing at a cash register, I』m holding a credit card, and I』m bored.


**Cashier:** Oh, I love your neighborhood. There』s a great gym right around the corner from your building.


**Ross:** That』s my gym.


**Cashier:** I can tell you work out. A paleontologist who works out, you』re like Indiana Jones.


**Ross:** I am like Indiana Jones.


**词汇短语点睛：**


Do you uh, want these things delivered Mr. and Mrs. Geller?


want something done 指的是「要别人做某事」，注意 deliver 用了被动式，这里收银员问的就是是否需要提供送货服务。


We are having a baby together, but we』re not involved. 


这里 involved 表示「与...关系亲密」，常常用来指两个人出于恋爱中。


we were seeing each other a while ago


see each other 可以表示恋人之间的「约会」。


And then there was one drunken night.


drunk 和 drunken 都可以表示「喝醉的」，形容一个喝醉酒的人就可以说 a drunk man，drunken 这里形容的是 night，表达的意思就是那晚「我们都喝醉了」。


Or, yes stranger, we』d like this delivered please.


这里的 or 从语气上表达就是转换到另一个话题，we'd 就是 we would 的缩写，like this delivered 和之前的 want these things delivered 是一个句式。


Why don』t you fill out this address card.


字面意思是「为什么你不填写一下地址？」，但实际在口语中 why don't you...并没有那种质问的语气，就是简单的表达「那就填一下地址吧。」


I noticed you picked out a lot of our dinosaur items.


item 指的就是一件商品，通常指的是在某个单子或列表中的商品。


Actually, that』s one of the reasons why we』re not a couple.


one of the reasons why 是口语里常见的说法，表达的就是「...的原因之一」，有时也可省略 why。


Oh yeah, don』t get too worked up over it.


work up 指一个人情绪激动，这句表达的含义就是「别因为这事儿（Ross 是古生物学家）兴奋激动过了头。」


I love your neighborhood. 


neighborhood 指的就是一个人住的街区、街坊。


There』s a great gym right around the corner from your building.


这里的 right 就是强调了健身房就在 Ross 住的地方出来的拐角处。


That』s my gym.


这里并不是说健身房是 Ross 开的，实际表达的就是「我就在那里健身。」


I can tell you work out. 


work out 在口语里可以表示「健身」


**地道句型活用：**


Actually, that』s one of the reasons why we』re not a couple.


one of the reasons（why）… 是口语里常见的说法，表达的就是「...的原因之一」。


I』m fascinated by paleontology.


be fascinated by 意思是「为...而着迷，极感兴趣的」。


**tell 绝不是你想的那么简单**


tell 这个词除了「告诉；告知」的意思之外，还有很多用法，比如本课对话末尾，收银员对 Ross 说 I can tell you work out. 这句话的意思是「我看得出来你有健身。」这里的 tell 有「知道；分辨」的含义。其他的一些常见用法：


tell the difference（区分，区别）


tell sb's fortune (tell fortunes)（给...算命）


you can never tell（谁也没法预料）


there is no telling（不可能知道）
