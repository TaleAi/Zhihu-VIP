## 48.死要面子：语气加强让你的英语有生命｜讲解
【本课对话取自第八季第 17 集】


**场景 1： Ross 问大家有没有看到他最爱的粉红色衬衫，之后推测一定是落在了 Mon**a 家。


![img](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-42ebfcf65d177efb8712299e1d24a1bb.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Ross:** Hey! Has anyone seen my shirt? It』s a button down, like a, like a faded salmon color?


**Monica:** You mean your pink shirt?


**Ross:** Faded salmon color.


**Monica:** No, I-I haven』t seen your pink shirt.


**Ross:** Great! Great. Then I must』ve left it at Mona』s. I knew it!


**Chandler:** Well, I』m sure you can get another one at *Ann Taylor』s*.


**Ross:** That』s my favorite shirt! Okay? I love that shirt!


**Rachel:** Well just ask Mona to give it back!


**Ross:** I don』t know. I mean I-I guess I could. It』s just we didn』t really end things on such good terms. And if I went over there I』d be ignoring the one thing she asked me to do when we broke up, jump up my own ass and die.


**场景 2：拒绝 Joey 表白后，Rachel 和 Joey 两人相处有些尴尬，Rachel 担心后面和 Joey 都没法共处一室了。**


![img](https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-102e8e454450358292007fcf6c1bfb6d.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Rachel:** So I thought Joey and I would be okay once we hung out, but it』s like we don』t even know how to be with each other anymore.


**Chandler:** I know it』s tough now, but things will get better.


**Rachel:** How do you know that? What if it just gets worse and worse and worse, to the point where we can』t even be in the same room with each other?


**Chandler:** I』m not great at the advice. Can I interest you in a sarcastic comment? Some cheese.


**词汇短语点睛：**


It』s a button down, like a, like a faded salmon color?


button down shirt 指的是领口带有纽扣，可以扣紧的衬衣。 faded 指的是「已褪色的；变淡的」。


Great! Great. 


Ross 这里说的是反话，实际表达的含义是「这下可好了！」


I must』ve left it at Mona』s.


意思是「我一定把落在 Mona 家了。」 Mona's 表示的就是 Mona 家。


I』m sure you can get another one at Ann Taylor』s.


Chandler 这说的是「我想你可以到 Ann Taylor 专卖店再买一件。」Ann Taylor 是一个女装品牌，Ann Taylor』s 指的就是这个品牌的店。


It』s just we didn』t really end things on such good terms. 


on good terms 指的是「关系好」，Ross 说的是他和 Mona 并不是和和气气分手的。


And if I went over there I』d be ignoring the one thing she asked me to do when we broke up, jump up my own ass and die.


这里 Ross 是假设「如果我到 Mona 那儿去」，the one thing 表达的是「Mona 特地交代我要做的一件事情。」


So I thought Joey and I would be okay once we hung out, but it』s like we don』t even know how to be with each other anymore.


hang out 指的是好朋友之间一起打发时间， 闲逛。 how to be with each other 指的就是「如何和对方相处」。这里的 with 就是「和某人在一起，和某人相处」的意思。


I know it』s tough now, but things will get better.


touch 指的是」困难的；棘手的「，things will get better 就是表示」一切都会好起来的。「 注意像 thing 这样的高频小词，本课对话中已经出现过多次。


I』m not great at the advice. 


Chandler 意思就是「我不擅长给建议。」也就是给不了什么好的建议。


Can I interest you in a sarcastic comment? 


sarcastic comment 指的就是类似第一段对话中，他嘲讽 Ross 粉红色衬衫的那种讽刺、挖苦的话。


**地道句型活用：**


What if ...


表达的含义是」假使…将会怎么样/该怎么办「。


to the point where ...


指的是「到...的程度」。Rachel 说的就是如果一切变得越来越糟糕，到后来可能她和 Joey 都无法共处一室。


**语气加强让你的英语有生命**


第一段对话中，Ross 说到 It』s just we didn』t really end things on such good terms. 这里要注意的是 just 放在从句之前所表达的那种加强的语气。英语中常用 It』s not ...   It』s just ... 来表示前后对比和强调。我们也可以通过这样的造句来刻意训练 It's just 后面那种强调的感觉。比如 Ross 的这句话也可以表达成 It's not that I don't want to ask Mona to give it back. It』s just we didn』t really end things on such good terms.
