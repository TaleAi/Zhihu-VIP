## 72.妈妈逼婚：be doing 未必是正在做某事｜讲解
**场景 1：Mrs. Geller 把 Ross 外婆的订婚戒指交给 Ross，希望他和 Rachel 能结婚。Ross 无奈先收下了戒指。**


![img](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-62d6e71748e0e2e427024d4f202478db.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Ross:** Hi! I』m so glad you』re here, but it』s gonna be a while. I-I wish you』d called first.


**Mrs. Geller:** Oh that』s all right, I』m coming back later with your father.


**Ross:** Oh good.


**Mrs. Geller:** I actually needed to talk to you before the birth.


**Ross:** Okay, what』s up?


**Mrs. Geller:** I brought something that I want to give you, assuming of course that you want it.


**Ross:** Ma, you』re asking me to marry you?


**Mrs. Geller:** This is your grandmother』s engagement ring, I want you to give it to Rachel.


**Ross:** Mom no, come on! Thank you.


**Mrs. Geller:** Just hear me out!


**Ross:** N-no! Okay? We』ve been through this! We』re not gonna get married just because she』s pregnant, okay?


**Mrs. Geller:** Honestly! Ross, this isn』t just some girl you picked up in a bar and humped. A child should have a family.


**Ross:** Mom, y』know what? I-I can』t deal with this right now. I』m sorry…


**Mrs. Geller:** Just…think about it. If you don』t, I』ll talk more about humping.


**Ross:** Gimmie!


**场景 2： Ross 和朋友讲述自己妈妈刚才来找他逼婚的事儿。**


![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-4c460f6726095a8071ac600116ad5509.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Ross:** She came and dragged me out of the labor room to ask me why I』m not with Rachel.


**Phoebe:** Yeah. Why aren』t you with Rachel?


**Ross:** Are you kidding? Look, we』re not gonna be together just because we』re having a baby. Okay?


**Phoebe:** But y』know what? It just seems that you two belong together.


**Ross:** Okay, stop it! I can』t deal with this right now. I have to go have a baby.


**Phoebe:** Right. And with who again? 


**词汇短语点睛：**


I』m so glad you』re here, but it』s gonna be a while.


be a while 这里指的是 Rachel 把小孩生下来还需要点时间。


I wish you』d called first.


这里 you』d 就是 you had 的缩写，Ross 的意思就是「你该先打个电话来。」


I actually needed to talk to you before the birth.


注意这里 needed 这里用了过去式，表达的含义就是「我之前就想过找你聊一聊」。这里用过去式其实客观上更加精确，因为一个人在和对方说「我想和你说...」，一般不是当下的决定，而是之前就想过。


This is your grandmother』s engagement ring, I want you to give it to Rachel.


engagement ring 指的是「订婚戒指」。


Ross, this isn』t just some girl you picked up in a bar and humped. 


pick up 这里指的是「随便地勾搭(异性)」。


I can』t deal with this right now. 


Ross 这里意思就是「我现在无法处理，无法面对这样的一件事情。」


Gimmie!


give me 在口语中可以缩读成 gimmie。


She came and dragged me out of the labor room to ask me why I』m not with Rachel.


drag out 指的是「把…拖[拉]出」，Ross 这里很形象地描述了自己目前把他从待产室里叫出来，not with Rachel 指的就是「不和 Rachel 在一起（结婚）」。


Are you kidding? 


相当于 Are you kidding me？意思就是「你和我开欢笑吧？」


We』re not gonna be together just because we』re having a baby.


注意这里 We' re not...just because...这样一个结构，表达的意思就是「我们不会就因为...就..."，前面对话中 Ross 也用过这个表达：We』re not gonna get married just because she』s pregnant.


**地道句型活用：**


Just hear me out!


意思是「听我把话说完！」另外，有时也可以用 let me finish 来表达。


We』ve been through this!


字面意思是「我们已经经历过这个了。」，结合语境，Ross 的意思就是「我们之前已经讨论过这个问题了。（不想再谈了）」


**be** **doing 未必是正在做某**事


be doing sth. 一般情况下是表示正在做的事，但本课对话中，Ross 母亲说到 I』m coming back later with your father. 这里 be coming back 并不是正在进行的动作，而是表示将来，意思就是 I will come back，这里 be doing 的用法就是表示「将要」去做某事。动词如果是短暂性动词，如 come, arrive, stay, leave, move 等，be doing sth. 就可以表将来。
