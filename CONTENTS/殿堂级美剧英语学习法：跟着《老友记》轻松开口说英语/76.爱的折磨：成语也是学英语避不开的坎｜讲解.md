## 76.爱的折磨：成语也是学英语避不开的坎｜讲解
【本课对话取自第八季第 24 集】


**场景：Phoebe 和 Ross 在婴儿房外聊了起来，Ross 说 Emma 长相优点都来自 Rachel，Phoebe 试图再一次建议 Ross 和 Rachel 应该结婚。**


![img](https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-072ff31e7393d15a87ae10bb8cead8d8.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Phoebe:** She in there?


**Ross:** Yeah. She』s putting her down now, that』s her.


**Phoebe**: Oh!


**Ross:** Look at Emma!


**Phoebe:** Just can』t decide who she looks more like, you or Rachel?


**Ross:** Oh what, are you kidding? She』s gorgeous. It』s all Rachel.


(Pause)


**Phoebe:** I』m sorry, for the last time, why aren』t you two together again? No, I know. I know, because you』re not in that place, which would be fine, except you totally are.


**Ross:** It』s…it』s complicated okay?


**Phoebe:** Yeah that』s true. Yeah, you love her. You always have. You have a child together. There is no right answer.


**Ross:** Look, we』ve been together. Okay? And then apart, and then together, and then apart, and now we have a baby. It』s just if-if we got together again and it didn』t work out…I could never do that to Emma. I mean she-she came into this world thinking everything—(Starts to cry.) Oh that』s…now me. What? Do they put something in the water in this place? Since Rachel and I we』re doing, we』re doing really well right now.


**Phoebe:** I know. I know. I know. I know, and if you try to make it more you might wreck it.


**Ross:** Yeah, exactly.


**Phoebe:** Right. (Pause) Or you might get everything you』ve wanted since you were fifteen.


**词汇短语点睛：**


She』s putting her down now, that』s her.


put down 就是「把...放下」，这里说的就是护士把 Emma 放到婴儿床上。


She』s gorgeous. 


gorgeous 常被用来形容一个人长得好看、非常漂亮迷人。另外也可以用来形容任何美丽的事物，比 beautiful 和 pretty 要高级一些。


It』s all Rachel.


字面意思是「她完全就是 Rachel」，实际意思就是遗传了 Rachel 的长相。


I』m sorry, for the last time, why aren』t you two together again?


for the last time 这里就表示「最后再问你一遍」。


No, I know. I know, because you』re not in that place, which would be fine, except you totally are.


not in that place 这里的意思是 Ross 还没有准备好，他的心思还没有在考虑这件事儿。


Yeah that』s true. Yeah, you love her. You always have. You have a child together. There is no right answer.


这句 Phoebe 在前面做了很长的铺垫，最后说 There is no right answer.实际要表达的意思，「你一直这么爱 Rachel，现在又有了 Emma，这还不足以让你决定和 Rachel 结婚吗？「


We』re doing really well right now.


这里 Ross 的意思就是「现在我和 Rachel 的关系处理得很好。」


I know. I know. I know. I know, and if you try to make it more you might wreck it.


wreck 有「毁坏；破坏」的意思，这里表达就是 Ross 和 Rachel 如果再往前发展，那目前相处的这种关系就「毁掉了」。


Or you might get everything you』ve wanted since you were fifteen.


这里 Phoebe 还是希望 Ross 和 Rachel 复合，这样 Ross 就可以得到他 15 岁就梦想的生活，因为 Ross 早在上学的时候就暗恋 Rachel。


**地道句型活用：**


Just can』t decide who she looks more like, you or Rachel?


decide 除了表示「决定」，这里的用法不是决定去做某事，而是表示「想好，判断出」。she looks more like 指的就是「她长得更像...」。


And then apart, and then together, and then apart, and now we have a baby.


Ross 和 Rachel 之前分分合合，apart 这里就是指两人分开、分手；口语中表示分分合合还可以用 on and off。


**成语也是学英语避不开的坎**


本课对话中，Ross 在 Phoebe 面前哭了之后，为了避免尴尬，说了一句 Do they put something in the water in this place? 字面意思是「这家医院他们是不是在水里放了东西？」，就说是不是水里有催泪的东西。这句话中间 something in the water 本身在英语里还可以用来表示解释不清楚的一些原因，例如 There must be something in the water. 就有「这里面一定有什么原因」的含义。另外，和 water 相关的成语还有很多，比如 take to something like a duck to water （如鱼得水），like a fish out of water（在陌生、不舒服或尴尬的环境），in deep water（遇上大麻烦，陷入困境），not hold water（不合理；站不住脚）等等。我们在遇到这类表达的时候，往往要透过字面意思去了解它的深层含义、引申含义，然后结合上下文去理解和熟悉。
