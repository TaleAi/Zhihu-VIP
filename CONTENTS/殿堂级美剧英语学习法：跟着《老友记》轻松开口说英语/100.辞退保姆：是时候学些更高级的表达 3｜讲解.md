## 100.辞退保姆：是时候学些更高级的表达 3｜讲解
【本课对话取自第九季第 6 集】


**场景：男保姆 Sandy 的娘娘腔让 Ross 几乎无法接受，最终还是和 Rachel 商议把他辞退掉。**


![img](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-693d61f36efacce28669fc3330a7ca4f.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Ross:** Sandy... Hi, we uhm... we kinda need to talk. I'm afraid it's not working out.


**Sandy:** Oh...


**Ross:** Yeah, uhm... I mean, Rachel and I, think you are great... with Emma... uhm... We just feel...


**Rachel:** YOU! You feel!


**Ross:** I... just feel that the... the chemistry isn't right. I'm sorry. We're... we're more than happy to give you a good recommendation...


**Sandy:** Oh, no, no, no... That's okay. I had a lot of offers from other families. I just picked you guys because... I liked you the best.


**Rachel:** Oh, damn you Geller!


**Ross:** Anyway, uhm...Well, I'm glad there's no hard feelings.


**Sandy:** No, none at all. You need to be happy with whoever is in your home... Although if you don't mind telling me, what was your problem? Maybe it's something I can work on in the future.


**Ross:** No, you know, it's uhm... nothing you did, it's... it's uhm... my issue.


**Sandy:** What is it...? *(Ross hesitates)* Please...?


**Ross:** You know, I'm just not uhm... that comfortable with a guy who's as sensitive as you.


**Sandy:** That's fair.


 


**词汇短语点睛：**


We kinda need to talk.


kinda 是 kind of 的缩写。


 


I mean, Rachel and I, think you are great... with Emma... 


这里 great with Emma 表达的含义就是「很会照顾 Emma」。


 


I... just feel that the... the chemistry isn't right.


the chemistry isn't right 字面意思是「化学反应不正确」，实际说的是两个人之间的关系相处得不够融洽。


 


I had a lot of offers from other families.


offer 字面意思有「出价、报价」的意思。在求职过程中，代表的就是公司原意支付你的薪资。找工作拿到 offer 指的是找工作得到录用了。


 


Well, I'm glad there's no hard feelings.


hard feeling 指的是「不愉快的情绪」。


 


Maybe it's something I can work on in the future.


work on sth 有「在...方面努力改善」的含义。


 


It's uhm... my issue.


issue，problem 都可以翻成「问题」，区别是 problem 往往还有「麻烦事；棘手的问题」的含义，issue 可以指「重要议题；争论的问题」，在多数口语里，例如这里 Ross 说的 issue 指「小问题」是一个比 problem 更柔和的词。


 


I'm just not uhm... that comfortable with a guy who's as sensitive as you.


that 这里是表示程度，英文里的 sensitive 和中文的「敏感」有些不同。中文更多指「脆弱，玻璃心」。这里对话中提到的 sensitive 形容的是一个人情绪起伏变化快，有点像女人一样的男人。


 


That's fair enough.


这句的意思就是「我能理解。」另外还有一句很口语化的表达「Fair enough.」 也是用来表示对方说的话很合理，让人理解和接受。


 


**地道句型活用：**


We're... we're more than happy to give you a good recommendation...


more than happy to do sth 表示「非常乐意去做某事」。


 


**是时候学些更高级的表达（三）**


本期我们来聊聊 work out 的用法，对话中 Ross 说到一句 I'm afraid it's not working out.这里的 work out 表达的意思是「进展顺利」，例如 We broke up because things didn't work out between us.（我们分手了，因为我们之间没戏。）。


另外 work out 还有 3 个常见的用法：


表示「健身，锻炼身体」，一般是是指去健身房的那种锻炼。


John works out in the gym two or three times a week.


John 每周在健身房锻炼两三次。


 


表示「解决，起作用」


It cost 5 million dollars to work out the problem.


解决该问题花了 500 万美元。


 


表示「结果是；算下来」


The trip worked out cheaper than we』d expected.


这次旅行花的钱比我们预计的要少。
