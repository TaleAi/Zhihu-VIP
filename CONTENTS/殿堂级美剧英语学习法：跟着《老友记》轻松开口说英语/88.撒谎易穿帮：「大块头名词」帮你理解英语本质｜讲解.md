## 88.撒谎易穿帮：「大块头名词」帮你理解英语本质｜讲解
【本课对话取自第九季第 3 集】


**场景：Joey 和 Phoebe 原本计划互为对方做媒，然后两对一起约会。结果 Joey 差点忘得一干二净，只好临时在咖啡厅抓了个生人 Mike 去。**


![img](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-cbc5963a96c7ed501947fa784a567393.webp)

**对话文本：** 


**Mike:** I gotta tell you, I can't believe I'm doing this with you. Although I did just get out of a nine-year relationship, so I guess I should be open to taking some risks.


**Joey:** Everything is gonna be fine. Just follow my lead, okay? All you have to do is pretend to be Mike.


**Mike:** I am Mike.


**Joey:** Atta boy!


**Joey:** Okay, look...


**Phoebe:** Joey, this is Mary Ellen Jenkins. So, Mike, how do you and Joey know each other anyway?


**Mike:** How do I and Joey know each other? Wow, if I had a nickel for every time somebody has asked me that.


**Joey:** From school.


**Mike:** Yeah, we met in college. I mean, high school.


**Phoebe:** Wow, you guys go way back then. So what are you up to these days?


**Mike:** Well, I'm a lawyer.


**Joey:** Mike, 'attorney at law'!


**Mike:** Actually, I just gave up my practice.


**Joey:** What? That's the kind of thing you usually run by me.


**Mike:** I always wanted to play piano professionally, and I figured if I don't do this now, I never will.


**Phoebe:** Wow, that's great! I liked that better than the lawyer thing, so...


**Joey:** Which is why I waited until now to introduce you to Mike.


**Mary Ellen:** I thought you thought he was still a lawyer.


**Joey:** No, no, that's not what I meant. Let's get you a cocktail.


**词汇短语点睛：**


I gotta tell you,...


gotta 是"got to"的缩写，是口语简化的表达。


Although I did just get out of a nine-year relationship, so I guess I should be open to taking some risks.


get out of sth 本身有「从...出来」的含义，这里 get out of a nine-year relationship 就表示「结束了一段 9 年的恋爱」。


Everything is gonna be fine. 


意思是「没事儿的。」这里是 Joey 想让 Mike 放心。


Just follow my lead, okay? 


follow one's lead 有「听从某人」的意思。这里 Joey 的意思就是让 Mike 到时候看他的指示。


Atta boy! 


口语里表示赞同、鼓励等的感叹语，意思就是「棒!很出色! 好样的!」


So, Mike, how do you and Joey know each other anyway?


这里 Phoebe 问 Mike「你跟 Joey 是怎么认识的呢？」


Wow, you guys go way back then. 


go way back.这里的意思是是「交情很深；认识很久了」。 


Mike, 'attorney at law'!


attorney at law 指的就是「律师」，是比较正式的说法。


Actually, I just gave up my practice.


practice 除了有「练习」的意思，还可以专门指医生和律师的业务。这句中 my practice 就是 my law practice。


That's the kind of thing you usually run by me.


run sth by sb 意思是「把某件事告诉某人以得到理解、征求意见」。这里 Joey 的意思就是「这种事你应该先跟我讨论的。」


I figured if I don't do this now, I never will.


意思就是「如果现在不赶快做，以后也不会做（就来不及了）。」


Which is why I waited until now to introduce you to Mike.


这里 Joey 对 Phoebe 说「这就是为什么我等到现在才介绍 Mike 给你。」这里的 which 指的就是 Phoebe 前面说 Wow, that's great! I liked that better than the law thing。


That is not what I mean.


这句的意思是「那不是我的意思。」


**地道句型活用：**


All you have to do is pretend to be Mike.


All you have to do is... 在口语中常用，意思是「你所要做的只是...」。


Wow, if I had a nickel for every time somebody has asked me that.


If I had a nickel for every time...是由 If I had a nickel for every time...,I'd be rich.这句话而来，字面意思是「如果（做某事）每次都能有五分钱拿的话，我就发财了。」而 nickel 是很小的钱币单位，若靠每次拿个五分钱就能发财，Mike 的这句引申表达的含义就是「这个问题被问过太多次了。」


So what are you up to these days?


what are you up to 问的就是「最近做些什么，忙些什么啊？做哪一行啊？」


 


**「大块头名词」帮你理解英文本质**


本课对话中，Mike 告诉 Joey 自己刚结束一段 9 年的恋爱，说到「I guess I should be ope**n to taking some r**isks.」这里 be open to sth 就是表示「接受...」后面的 sth 是一个名词的概念，如果后面跟的是一段话的话，就要把它「打包」成一个名词的概念，就像一个「大块头名词」，相当于一个名词化的模块，在这句话中的「大块头名词」就是「 taking some risks（冒一些风险）」。类似的还比如 I'm looking f**orward to** seeing you.。 另外像 where/what/who/why/which/how 后接的陈述短句，也是「大块头名词」的感觉，我们在说的时候，头脑中要把它当做一个整体模块来练习。 比如 Let **me show you what this c**amera can do.
