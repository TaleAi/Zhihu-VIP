## 6.家务能手：英文里小词有大威力｜讲解
**场景：Monica 回到家发现家里被打扫过，原以为是 Chandler 的功劳，却得知是因为 Chandler 请了个钟点工，这可让 Monica 有点不适应了。**


![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-c97ba511d382dbbdc83acbbafc7f2c74.webp)

对话文本：


**Monica:** Oh my God! You cleaned! Look at these floors! You did the windows! Oh, I have been begging you for months and you did! You cleaned! And nagging works!


**Chandler:** Y』know uh, I didn』t actually do this.


**Monica:** Oh no, was I cleaning in my sleep again?


**Chandler:** No, it wasn』t you.


**Monica:** Well then who?


**Chandler:** I got a maid. Yay!


**Monica:** Well, I hope by maid you mean mistress, because if some other woman was here cleaning then…


**Chandler:** Uh honey, I know you don』t like to relinquish control…


**Monica:** Oh, relinquish is just a fancy word for lose!


**Chandler:** Look, she』s really nice. Okay? And she mentioned that she adored the way that you arranged the sponges.


**Monica:** Did she really say that?


**Chandler:** Yes, I distinctly remember 『cause I thought it was a joke. Now just give her a chance, okay?


**Monica:** Fine, I can do it. Whew.


**Chandler:** What』s the matter?


**Monica:** Well, usually when I』m this anxious, I clean!


 


**词汇短语点睛：**


You cleaned!


Monica 在说这句话的时候，cleaned 结尾的/d/发音很清楚，注意英美母语者在说这句话的时候，考虑的不是过去时态，不是语法，而是脑海中的图像、情境触发脱口而出这句英文。


begging


除了「乞讨; 行乞」，口语中常表示「恳求」的意思。


nagging


通常指唠叨; ;不断地抱怨、挑剔或批评（某人），对话中指的就是 Monica 觉得平时对 Chandler 的唠叨还是管用的。 


Was I cleaning in my sleep again?


对话中是 Monica 问 Chandler 是不是自己在梦游的时候把家里打扫了，以后如果要描述「梦游」里做了什么，要学会简单地使用短语「in one's sleep」。


relinquish 


有「放弃」的意思，往往指的是不情愿地放弃，Monica 在对话中认为 relinquish 就是「失败」（lose)的一种「华丽」（fancy）的版本。


sponge 


指洗涤用的海绵块，也可以做动词用，表示用海绵或湿布擦。


distinctly remember


表示「清楚地记得」。 


  



**地道句型活用：**


**小词也有大威力：万能词「do」**


Monica 对 Chandler 说 You did the windows! 注意 do 这个小词的活用。「do」是一个用法相当广泛的词，下次，当你很痛苦于不知道该用哪个动词时，可以试试用这个万能动词「do」。例如：


Go do your homework!


快去做你的家庭作业。


Have you done your teeth? 


你刷过牙了吗？


Who will do the cooking tomorrow? 


明天谁做饭？


I'll do a translation for you.  


我来帮你翻译。


Is your report done? 


你的报告写完了吗？


  



What do you mean by…?


课文对话中 Monica 说到 I hope by maid you mean mistress. 口语中常常会想表达「你刚说的…是指什么？/你说的…是什么意思？」 英文是 What do you mean by…?是一个询问意思或意图的常用句型，比如如果对方说的话中有个单词你不知道什么意思（比如 relinquish），那你也可以这样询问：What do you mean by 「relinquish」, and can you spell it? 当然如果直接说 What do you mean? （你这是什么意思？）往往是带有恼火、质问的口气。
