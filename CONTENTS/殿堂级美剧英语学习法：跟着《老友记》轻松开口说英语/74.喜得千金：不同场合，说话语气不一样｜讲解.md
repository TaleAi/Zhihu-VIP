## 74.喜得千金：不同场合，说话语气不一样｜讲解
【本课对话取自第八季第 24 集】


**场景： Rachel 的女儿出生了，大伙儿在讨论给她取什么名字，Monica 将小时候为以后自己孩子取的名字送给了 Rachel。**

**对话文本：** 


**Chandler:** So, do you know what you』re gonna call her yet?


**Phoebe:** Oh, wait a minute. It』s not gonna be Baby Girl? I thought that was so original!


**Ross:** Uh actually, we-we』ve narrowed it down to two names.


**Rachel:** Yeah, and y』know what? I love them both, so why don』t you just pick one and that』ll be it.


**Ross:** Wow! Umm, okay uh…everyone…this…is Isabella. What?


**Rachel:** That』s not her name! I』m sorry, she just doesn』t feel like an Isabella.


**Joey:** What was the other one Ross?


**Ross:** Umm, Delilah.


**Rachel:** Oh great! Suddenly she sounds like a biblical whore.


**Ross:** So I guess we』re back to uh, Baby Girl.


**Phoebe**: Yay!


**Rachel:** Well what are we gonna do?


**Monica:** It』s okay honey, you』ll find a name.


**Ross:** Ugh, easy for you to say, you already know what your kids』 names are gonna be.


**Chandler**: You do?


**Monica:** Yeah, I』ve had them picked out since I was fourteen.


**Chandler:** Oh no, it』s gonna be named after some snack or baked good, isn』t it?


**Rachel:** Well tell us! What are they?


**Monica:** Umm, okay. If it』s a boy it』s Daniel.


**Rachel:** And if it』s a girl?


**Monica:** I don』t want to say.


**Rachel:** Oh, just tell us! We』re not gonna want it!


**Monica:** Okay. It』s Emma.


**Rachel:** Emma! See? I don』t want it.


**Monica:** Take it.


**Rachel**: What?


**Monica:** She』s clearly an Emma.


**Rachel:** Oh honey, but you love that name.


**Monica:** Yeah, but I love you more. Besides y』know, nothing goes with Bing. So I』m screwed.


**词汇短语点睛：**


So, do you know what you』re gonna call her yet?


这里的 call 就是「给…取名」的意思。


I thought that was so original!


Phoebe 比较得意自己起的名字。original 这里表示「有创意的，新颖的」。


Uh actually, we-we』ve narrowed it down to two names.


narrow 做动词指「使...变窄」，这里 narrow sth down to 的意思就是「把范围缩小至…」。


I love them both, so why don』t you just pick one and that』ll be it.


Rachel 表示两个名字都喜欢，that'll be it 这里意思就「那就那样了。」这里表示将来，即等 Ross 选好其中一个名字后，就用这个名字。


I』m sorry, she just doesn』t feel like an Isabella.


这里 Rachel 的意思是「她感觉上不像是 Isabella。」feel like 这里表示「给人的感觉..."。


So I guess we』re back to uh, Baby Girl.


back to 表示「回到了」，回到的是之前 Phoebe 的「创意」，这里 Ross 的意思就是「那我们还是叫她 Baby Girl 吧。」


I』ve had them picked out since I was fourteen.


这里 had them picked out 表达的就是「这些名字已经被选好了」，但没有特别强调是谁选的。have sth done 本身的含义就是「让人做了某事」。


She』s clearly an Emma.


这句字面意思「她显然就是 Emma」，Monica 的意思就是 Rachel 的女儿很合适叫 Emma 这个名字。


Besides y』know, nothing goes with Bing. So I』m screwed.


go with 可以表示「与...相配，搭配」的意思，这里 Monica 的意思就是去什么名字好像都和 Chandler 的姓 Bing 不搭。screwed 作为形容词使用，表达「in serious trouble」，即「碰上大麻烦了」之意，意思就是「我完蛋了，我死定了」


**地道句型活用：**


easy for you to say...


表示「你说的倒容易...」，这里是口语中的省略，完整的说法是 It is easy for you to say。


Oh no, it』s gonna be named after some snack or baked good, isn』t it?


be named after 表示「以…命名」。例如 Some parents name their children after flowers.（有些父 母以花的名称来为小孩命名 。）


**不同场合，说话语气不一样**


Albert 在讲解中，复述对话内容的时候往往会放慢语速，为的是让大家听得更清楚一些，但我们要特别留意的是原声对话的语速和语气。特别是英语母语者在不同场合，说话的语气是不同的，比如进行正式的演讲，吐字往往会非常清楚，感情的投入也会非常到位，但在日常生活当中，语气就会显得随意。所以我们在学老友记练口语的时候，除了学习地道的表达，也要特别注意说话的语气，体会美国人在日常交流、寒暄中，在熟悉的朋友之间，他们说话的语气。这样我们在实际对话中，才会让对方听上去显得自然。
