## 5.津巴多：心理学的本质是什么？
Chapter 2 : In this second chapter, you may find its content is not that different than any other social science discipline, because we use similar research methods. Statistics and programming, are two important skills for researchers, as for any science studies when it concerns many people, as well as lots of data of all kinds.


This chapter talks about the research methods employed by psychologists. When I first wrote this chapter, I had to decide whether it should come early in my text or at the end after the reader and understood more about the many areas of psychological study.


I chose to have this chapter on research methods come early because it is critical that readers understand psychology is a science, and relies on scientific methods to demonstrate its conclusions. In a sense, we are using research of all kinds and statistics to help unlock the mysteries of human nature.


以上是津巴多教授的原音重现，下面我用中文为你转述他的讲解：你可能觉得第二章内容好像跟其他社会学科都差不多，因为这一章是关于研究方法的。统计学和编程，是心理学和其他科学都使用的最重要的两个技能，因为我们的研究会涉及大量的人，使用大量的数据。


这一章是关于心理学家常用的一些研究方法。写这一章的时候，我在考虑这章应该排到前面去，或者是放到后面，这样大家可以先了解些心理学的基础知识以后再学习研究方法。后来我决定还是放到前面来，因为这样能让读者理解心理学是一门科学，是用科学方法来得出结论的，这是很重要的一件事。说到底，心理学是用各种研究方法和统计学来探秘人性的。
