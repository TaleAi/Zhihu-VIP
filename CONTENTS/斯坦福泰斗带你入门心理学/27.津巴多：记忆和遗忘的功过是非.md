## 27.津巴多：记忆和遗忘的功过是非
This memory chapter triggers my personal concerns for memory losses as I have begun to age into my 80 decade. However I was encouraged by new researches that we all can not only sustain our memories over time but also enhance memories with practical skills I have outlined here. I also like the final section where I present much new research on the biological aspects of memory. 


关于记忆的这一章引发了我本人关于记忆损失的担忧，我毕竟已经 80 多岁了。不过，新的研究也是很鼓舞人心的，不光是我们可以更久地保持记忆，我们也可以通过本书里列出的一些实际技巧来增强记忆。最后一个章节是我比较喜欢的，我在里面展示了不少记忆的生理方面的新研究。
