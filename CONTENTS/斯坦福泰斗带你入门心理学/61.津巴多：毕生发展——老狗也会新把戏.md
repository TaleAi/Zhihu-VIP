## 61.津巴多：毕生发展——老狗也会新把戏
My main problem on this chapter on human development across the lifespan is where in this textbook it should go. Some other textbooks put it at the beginning others assign it at later or almost final section. My decision was to put it in the center of this textbook because it can be used to integrate many of the things we have learned earlier and then to point us ahead to exciting domains of motivation, health and personality. Naturally I conclude this chapter with a new section on learning to age successfully, which, I think I am doing reasonably well at my age. 


对我来说，本章关于毕生发展的主要问题是，该把它放在教科书的哪一部分。其他一些教科书会放在开始，或者靠后的位置，有的干脆放在最后一章。我决定把它放在这本教科书的中间，因为它可以用来整合我们之前学到的许多东西，然后引向动机、健康和人格这些激动人心的领域。当然，因为是我写的么，我在本章的结尾部分加了一个关于如何学习成功进入老年的新章节，我自认为我在这个年龄段表现得还是相当不错的。
