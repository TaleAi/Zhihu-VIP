## 8.津巴多：心理学的「硬功夫」和「软功夫」
Chapter 3


 This chapter is about evolution, genetics, and about how our body and our mind work together to create the very basic foundation of our psychology. We will learn some basic concepts, such as neurotransmitters, hormones, neuroscience research, and much about that special 3-pound organ—our brain—that runs the whole show of our every action. 


These concepts will be brought up in future chapters repeatedly, and also, we should add that neuro-scientific research is now the most impactful trend in modern psychology. 


When I first wrote this chapter I knew very little about these 「hard psych」domains, because I am more a social-cognitive psychologist ，we will meet these more 「soft psych」 folks later on.


So, I first read all I could find about each of these topics, and then I met personally with my colleagues who were experts in each of these areas to learn why they have devoted their careers to understanding the brain and physiological processes.


They also advised me how I could present these ideas in interesting ways to new students of psychology, like you! I hope some of that excitement comes through to you.


以上是津巴多教授的原音重现，下面我用中文为你转述他的讲解：


第三章是关于进化，基因，以及我们的身体和精神是如何一起组成我们心理活动的基础的。
我们会学一些基础知识，比如神经递质，激素，脑神经研究，以及我们这个独特的有三磅重的器官，这个我们一切行为的幕后控制者：我们的脑。这些基础概念在未来的章节里也会不断出现，我也想让大家知道，脑神经科学的研究，是今天心理学的最重要的一个新趋势。我最早写这章时，对于这些偏「硬心理学」的领域并不熟知。我是一个社会认知心理学专家，也就是偏「软心理学」的专家。


所以，我就研究了所有这些话题，与这些领域的心理学同事交谈，理解为什么他们投身这些关于脑神经和人体生理过程方面的研究。他们给了我很多好建议，使得我能够以一种有趣的方法，把这些观点呈现给新的心理学学习者，比如你。我希望这些知识也能给你带来有趣的体验。
