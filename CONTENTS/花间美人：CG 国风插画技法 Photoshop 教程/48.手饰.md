## 48.手饰
手饰通常以戒指、手环为主。 


![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-c8158e7d59db0f2105cd865f531e371f_r.webp)

垂珠戒指，富贵人家的女子通常会佩戴    




  



![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-6904b51442d01b980c5c2964a6019d92_r.webp)

男子护腕和玉手环    




  



![img](https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a82b84227679bc150af24a88b8b30810_r.webp)

![img](https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-b2f2062b3fe8bc522e7278a6f81a1f3b_r.webp)
